Anubis (アヌビス神, Anubisu-shin, lit. "God Anubis") is the former Stand of Caravan Serai, and a tertiary antagonist featured in the third part of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure series, Stardust Crusaders, specifically the "Anubis" story arc. It is also a member of the Egypt 9 Glory Gods, a group of Stands representing ancient Egyptian gods.

Anubis is a Stand bound to an ancient sword with the ability to possess whoever holds it in body and mind, forcing them to become its user in the process. As it repeatedly attacks the Joestar Group, Anubis learns from its failures, becoming a stronger opponent with each defeat.

Survivor (サバイバー, Sabaibā) is the Stand granted to Guccio by Whitesnake, featured in the sixth part of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure series, Stone Ocean.

It appears as a group of small Stands that travel through water and increase the aggressiveness of anyone affected by their minute electrical signal. Out of all the Stands DIO knew, this one is said to be the weakest.

Weather Report (ウェザー・リポート, Wezā Ripōto), real name Wes Bluemarine (ウェス・ブルーマリン, Wesu Burūmarin), is a primary ally featured in the sixth part of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure series, Stone Ocean.

An amnesiac at his introduction, Weather Report is a fellow inmate at Green Dolphin Street Prison who seeks his past memory and allies himself with Jolyne Cujoh. Weather is a Stand user capable of controlling various weather phenomena through his ability, which is also named Weather Report.

Yo-Yo Ma (ヨーヨーマッ, Yō Yō Ma) is the Stand of D an G, featured in the sixth part of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure series, Stone Ocean.

It is an invulnerable Automatic Sentient Stand that, once ordered by the user, uses saliva to dissolve parts of its target. However, if more than one person is observing it, the Stand acts like a loyal servant and follows every command.
